# Work-Kinetic Energy Theorem
W $_{\text{ext}}$ = K $_f$ - K $_i$ = $\Delta K$

If the "System" is
- Just the book (F, gravity are external forces)
	- Going UP: F & gravity - zero net force = zero work
	- Down: work by gravity = kinetic energy
	- Each step must be considered separately
- Book + earth (F is external, gravity is internal)
	- Up: Work on system by F (external force) adds energy
	- Down: to original position, kinetic energy gained = work by F
		- F (external) added energy to the system (during UP)
		- Internal forces don't do work on (add energy to) the system at the down step the system's energy doesn't change
		- No change → a thread of continuity

# Elastic Potential Energy
System = block (mass m) + spring (constant k)
- equilibrium position x = 0
- block is moved (by an external force) to x=-x $_f$
- work done by external force (W) = potential energy gained by system = U $\text{spring}$
= $\frac{1}{2} kx^2_f$

Sign of potential energy for spring is opposite the sign of the work done by the force
