- system = "a small portion of the universe"
	- The focus of our problem, may be >>1 objects
- environment of system = everything else

The scalar product (aka "dot product") of two vectors
written as
$\overrightarrow{A} * \overrightarrow{B}$
is calculated as 
$\overrightarrow{A} * \overrightarrow{B} = ABcos\theta = A * B_x = A_x * B$
scalar (actually, 1d vector; can be + or -)

Mathematical properties
communitive (it doesn't matter which part comes first in multiplication)
distributive property

- Work, W, done on a system by an external force F
W = $F\Delta r cos\theta$
- Unit: joule (J)
	- 1 joule = 1 newton
	- J = N × m
- W is the scalar product
	- $W = \overrightarrow{F}* \Delta \overrightarrow{r}$
