- A freely falling object is any object moving freely under the influence of gravity alone, regardless of its initial motion. Objects thrown upward or downward and those released from rest are all falling freely once they are released. All freely falling objects under the same gravitation experience an acceleration directed downward and of the same magnitude regardless of its initial motion.
