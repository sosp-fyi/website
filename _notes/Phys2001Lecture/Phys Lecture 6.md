# Projectile Motion
Motion of an object moving in free fall (ignoring air resistance) in 2D (horizontal and vertical directions) is known as projectile motion
## Kinematic Equations
$$
\overrightarrow{v_f}=\overrightarrow{v_i}+\overrightarrow{a}t
$$
$$
	\overrightarrow{r_f}=\overrightarrow{r_i}+\overrightarrow{v_i}t+\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{a}t^2
$$
