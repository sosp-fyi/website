# Conservation of Angular Momentum
The total angular momentum of a system is constant if the resultant external torque acting on the system is zero.
$$
\overrightarrow L_f = \overrightarrow L_i
$$
$$
I_f \overrightarrow \omega_f = I_i \overrightarrow \omega_i
$$
