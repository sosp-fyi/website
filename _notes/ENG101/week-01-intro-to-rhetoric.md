Class discussion covered ethos, pathos, and logos as foundations for persuasive writing.

## Key Points

- Ethos builds trust with the audience.
- Pathos appeals to emotion and values.
- Logos relies on clear reasoning and evidence.

## Reminder

Bring one editorial example next class and identify all three rhetorical appeals.
