### Empirical and Molecular Formulas
empirical formula
	- simplest formula for a compound
	- shows relative numbers of atoms
	- smallest whole # ratio
e.g. - CH $_2$ O

molecular formula
	- actual numbers of atoms

e.g.. - C $_2$ H $_4$ O $_2$ (acetic acid)
	- C $_6$ H $_{12}$ O $_6$ (glucose)

### Strategy to Calculate an Empirical Formula
1. Get to moles: if you already have a ratio of mol amounts, move to step 2.
2. divide each of the mole amounts by the smallest mole amount. The new numbers are the subscripts in the empirical formula.
Special case:
If fractions are encountered, multiply by a common factor to provide whole numbers for each subscript
