# Reaction Types
- combination Reactions
	- reactants combine: X + Y -> Z
- Decomposition Reactions
	- compound decomposes: Z -> X + Y
- Displacement Reactions
	- double: AB + CD -> AC + BD (not redox)
	- single: X + YZ -> XZ + Y
	- (note how these are different from double displacement precipitation reactions - anions become cations, cations become anions)
- Combustion
	- process of combining with O $_2$

## The Activity Series of the Metals
- An element can reduce (i.e. be oxidized by) ions of elements below it on the scale.
- In other words, higher elements can reduce (i.e. be oxidized by) lower elements.
- A reaction will occur when the neutral metal (s) is higher on the scale than the cationic metal (+).

# Thermochemistry
When a chemical bond is a food or fuel molecule is broken, energy is absorbed.
Breaking bonds absorbs energy.
Creating bonds releases energy.

### Important Terminology
System: What we focus on (we define it)
Surroundings: Everything else which could change because of a change in the system
Internal Energy, E: Sum of all potential and kinetic energies of the particles in the system

### The Law of Energy Conservation
First law of thermodynamics: the total energy of the universe is constant.
Energy is neither created nor destroyed, but transferred as heat and/or work.
