# Tell Me about a time You Faced Difficulties with a Team Member, and how Did You Resolve That?
During my senior year capstone project where we had to create a viable product, our team leader wasn't completing all of his responsibilities and was decreasing team cohesion which was affecting our grade.

So, I pulled him aside and talked to him about it and in the end, I ended up taking on parts of his role so that our team would better succeed.

Due to this, when the time came to present our product to potential investors, we ended up getting the most "investment money" into our product.
