## Artificial Intelligence: Designing Smart Systems
AI name came about in the 50's late 50's

### Turing Test
Alan Turing in 1950 proposed a test for machine intelligence
- Natural language processing
- Knowledge representation
- Automated reasoning
- Machine learning

Total Turing test also includes:
- Computer vision
- Mechanical movements
Robotics captures all the above tests.

### Smart Systems
IBM's "Watson" - won the jeopardy championship. This system knew reasoning, knowledge, and answers questions
Towel finding robot - it can see, reasons/strategizes, takes action based off of that.
Robot soccer team - sees, reasons/strategizes, takes action
Flying robots play music - see, cooperate, reason, strategize, take actions
