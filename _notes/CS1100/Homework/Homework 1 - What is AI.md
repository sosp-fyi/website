## What is the Total Turing Test?

The Total Turing Test is an extension of the Turing Test created by Alan Turing in the 1950s. The original test only focused on natural language processing, knowledge representation, automated reasoning, and machine learning. These tests were solely focused on the cognitive side of machine intelligence. However, the Total Turing Test added both computer vision and mechanical movement. These additional tests further check the AI's ability to do cognitive reasoning as well as interact with the physical world in an analogous way to humans.
